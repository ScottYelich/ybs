# Business Specifications

This directory contains business specifications for the bootstrap system.

## Purpose

Business specifications describe the **business value** and **justification** for features, including:
- Problem statements and user stories
- Success metrics and ROI analysis
- Target users and personas
- Acceptance criteria
- Market analysis and competitive positioning
- Priority and urgency
- Cost-benefit analysis

## Template

Use `framework/templates/TEMPLATE-business.md` as the starting point for new business specifications.

## When to Create a Business Specification

Create a business specification when:
- Proposing a new feature or capability
- Justifying significant development effort
- Analyzing market fit or competitive positioning
- Defining success criteria for a major initiative
- Documenting user needs and pain points

## Related Specification Types

- **Functional** (`../functional/`) - User workflows implementing business requirements
- **Technical** (`../technical/`) - Implementation of functional requirements
- **Architecture** (`../architecture/`) - Architectural decisions supporting business goals
- **Testing** (`../testing/`) - Validation of business acceptance criteria
- **Security** (`../security/`) - Security requirements driven by business needs
- **Operations** (`../operations/`) - Operational support for business objectives

## Naming Convention

Business specifications should follow the pattern:
```
ybs-spec_<12-hex-guid>.md
```

Example: `ybs-spec_a1b2c3d4e5f6.md`
